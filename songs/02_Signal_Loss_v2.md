# Signal Loss — Rewritten (Album Track 2)

> Complete rewrite. Original was too generic — "scrolling through lives," "chasing likes and replies" 
> is every social media song ever written. This version makes it SPECIFIC to Vole-tek's world:
> a cybersecurity professional whose job is maintaining connections for everyone else
> while his own connections decay. The signal metaphor now runs deeper — he IS signal.

---

## STYLE PROMPT:
Dark cinematic alternative rock, emotional electronic rock hybrid, baritone male vocal, vocal range A2–E4, key D minor, atmospheric electric guitars, cinematic drums, ambient synth textures, emotional isolation themes, modern loneliness atmosphere, faded deep echoes, restrained vulnerable delivery, deep bass, soft glitch ambience, introspective cyber-noir tone, 80 BPM, 4/4, progressive signal decay arc, no autotune feel

[Exclusions: ‑autotune, ‑rap, ‑scream vocals, ‑vocal hums, ‑beatboxing, ‑bright synths, ‑upbeat energy, ‑trap drums, ‑cheerful melodies]

## PRODUCTION NOTES:

• Key: D minor — dark, cinematic, emotionally heavy, accommodates baritone low range
• Tempo: 80 BPM (slightly faster than ballad — working pace, mechanical rhythm)
• Time Signature: 4/4
• Chord Progression:
  Verse: i – VI – v – III (Dm – Bb – Am – F) [open, drifting, unresolved]
  Pre-Chorus: iv – VII – i – i (Gm – C – Dm – Dm) [tension → static]
  Chorus: i – VI – III – VII (Dm – Bb – F – C) [wide, emotional, cinematic]
  Bridge: i – i – iv – iv (Dm – Dm – Gm – Gm) [trapped, repetitive, no movement]
  Final Chorus: i – VI – VII – i (Dm – Bb – C – Dm) [circular, inevitable decay]
• Vocal: Male baritone (A2–E4), hollow → cracking → desperate echo
• Instruments: Sparse piano, ambient electric guitar (reverb-heavy), deep bass pulse, industrial heartbeat percussion, cinematic drums (chorus), glitch textures (progressive), soft synth pads
• Dynamics: Ambient hum (intro) → sparse hollow (V1) → filtered tension (PC) → wide emotional swell (C) → darker deeper (V2) → near-silence (bridge) → degrading full force (FC) → dissolving static (outro)
• Hook Type: Title hook — "Signal loss" repeated as mantra + "Green dot active but nobody's home" as secondary hook
• Rhyme Scheme: V=AABB (couplets — technical, clean), PC=ABCB (conversational), C=Free (emotional priority)
• Emotional Arc: Hollow routine → unacknowledged isolation → desperate reach → realization he IS the signal that's failing → degradation into the system
