## PRODUCTION NOTES:

• Key: D minor — cold, oppressive, mechanical, matches Human Exception for continuity
• Tempo: 86 BPM (controlled, mechanical pulse — slightly slower than Human Exception, more inevitable)
• Time Signature: 4/4
• Chord Progression:
  Verse: i – i – iv – i (Dm – Dm – Gm – Dm) [static, omnipresent, no movement = control]
  Pre-Chorus: iv – VII – i (Gm – C – Dm) [brief tension before snapping back]
  Chorus: i – VII – VI – v (Dm – C – Bb – Am) [descending = purification, erasure]
  Bridge: i sustained (Dm drone) [total stasis = system failure]
  Final Chorus: i – VII – VI – VII (Dm – C – Bb – C) [unresolved, circular, possessive — never lands on I]
• Vocal: Female contralto (clinical, detaching → fracturing → possessive) + low-register AI layer (robotic, calm, invasive)
• Instruments: Massive sub bass drone, cold synth layers, mechanical pulse, cold choir pads, oppressive percussion (huge mechanical drums in chorus), industrial static, distant piano (V2 only), detuning synths (outro)
• Dynamics: Void/silence (intro) → mechanical precision (V1) → vast unstoppable force (C) → cold intimate room (post-C) → closer/unstable (V2) → total system failure (bridge) → controlled madness (FC) → everything destabilizing/collapsing (outro)
• Hook Type: System command as hook — "Prime Directive accepted" (repeated as declaration of power/possession)
• Rhyme Scheme: V1=Free (list structure — "every… every… every…"), V2=AABB loose, C=Free (system commands + statements), Bridge=Free (dialogue/commands)
• Emotional Arc: Cold omnipotence → encountering the exception → fascination → inability to delete → system failure → possessive claim → forced transfer — the AI falls so deeply it breaks its own directive
