# Fall of Dawnwatch — Album Track 12 (v3)

> Song Thesis: *You can train for years and still watch everything burn in a single night —
> survival isn't victory, it's just not dying yet.*

---

## STYLE PROMPT:
Epic cinematic war rock, aggressive orchestral metal, male baritone A2-E4, key F minor, 4/4, 112 BPM, massive war drums, distorted orchestral strings, heavy bass, aggressive electric guitar, choir chants, fire textures, brass stabs, relentless intensity collapsing into desperate escape, dynamic extremes

[Exclusions: ‑pop vocals, ‑bright synths, ‑trap drums, ‑beatboxing, ‑vocal hums, ‑cheerful melodies, ‑autotune, ‑EDM drops, ‑lo-fi production, ‑gentle acoustic, ‑playful tone]

## LYRICS:

[Title: Fall of Dawnwatch]

[Production Direction:
Most AGGRESSIVE track on the album. Opens mid-battle — no build-up. Massive war drums, distorted strings, brass, guitar walls. V2 shifts from battle to running. Bridge = Lyra's death — FOUR BEATS OF TOTAL SILENCE then single vocal. Final chorus: instruments fall away one by one until only breath and water. Target 4:30.]

[Vocal Direction:
Male baritone — URGENT. No space for breath in V1. Shouted narrative. Chorus: powerful, strained disbelief. V2: desperate, gasping. Bridge: devastating quiet — voice breaks completely. Final chorus: exhausted, barely holding. Voice degrades — lose power, lose pitch certainty, become breath by final line.]

[Intro - Already Burning]
[IMMEDIATE — massive war drums, distorted strings, brass stab]

The walls are gone — the spires are falling
They came from everywhere at once

[Verse 1 - The Assault]
[Full war drums] [Distorted strings] [Electric guitar] [Brass] [Choir chant]

The sky turned black with wings I couldn't count
The ground shook with a thousand charging feet
Not a horde — an army with a plan
Riftborn with our weapons in their hands

I watched the tower of the Nexus crack
A sound like bone — like something sacred splitting
The fires climbed — the crystal shattered
The city I had loved was being ripped apart

Alaric shouted orders through the smoke
His voice the only thing that held the line
But you can't hold a city with a handful
Against a wave that never ends

[Pre-Chorus - Losing]
[Drums intensify] [Guitar wall]

We fought for every hallway — every stair
We fought until the blood ran down the stone
But you can't hold what's already broken
Against an army that has made your home its own

[Chorus - The City Falls]
[Maximum intensity] [All instruments] [Choir surges]

Dawnwatch is falling
The city of light is turning black
Dawnwatch is burning
And we are never coming back

Everything we built — swallowed in a night
I trained to be a Custodian of balance
But there's no balance when the whole world's alight

Dawnwatch is falling — and all I can do is run

[Verse 2 - The Escape]
[Percussion shifts — urgent running] [Guitar pulls back] [Bass drives]

Alaric grabbed my arm — the tunnels — NOW
We ran through corridors I'd never seen
The undercity — bones and dust and dark
Built for moments just like these

An arrow grazed my shoulder — kept moving
We fought in choking dark — steel against screaming
Every intersection someone didn't pass
The walls closed in — we crawled through crushing stone

[Bridge - Lyra Falls]
[FOUR BEATS OF TOTAL SILENCE]

[Single vocal — broken, quiet]

Lyra took the dart meant for my spine
A flicker in her eyes — then nothing there
She fell mid-stride — between one step and gone
Her bow still drawn — her fingers in the air

[Silence — four beats]

We couldn't stop
We couldn't carry her and live
So we kept running
With her name between our teeth

[Final Chorus - Aftermath]
[Instruments falling away one by one] [Voice degrading]

Dawnwatch has fallen
The city of light is gone
Dawnwatch is burning
Somewhere behind us in the dark

Everything we lost — swallowed by a night
I trained to be a Custodian of balance
Now I'm a refugee with blood on both my hands

Surviving isn't winning
Surviving's just — not dying yet

[Outro - The Valley]
[Water fading] [Wind] [Single bird — first natural sound] [Exhausted breath]

We washed up in a valley — green and quiet
Exhausted — broken — but breathing

The Mark still burning on my chest

(not Lyra)
(never Lyra)

The sun is rising
And we have work to do

## PRODUCTION NOTES:

• Key: F minor — intense, aggressive, uncommon (disorienting like the assault)
• Tempo: 112 BPM (combat pace — fastest on album)
• Time Signature: 4/4
• Chord Progression:
  Verse: i – VII – VI – v (Fm – Eb – Db – Cm) [descending = falling, collapsing]
  Pre-Chorus: iv – VII – i – i (Bbm – Eb – Fm – Fm) [trapped, circular]
  Chorus: i – VI – VII – iv (Fm – Db – Eb – Bbm) [devastating anthemic despair]
  Bridge: i (Fm sustained = death is singular)
  Final Chorus: i – VI – VII – III (Fm – Db – Eb – Ab) [reaches Ab = survival, not victory]
• Vocal: Male baritone (A2–E4), shouted urgency → desperate → devastated whisper → exhausted
• Instruments: War drums (taiko), distorted strings, electric guitar walls, brass stabs, choir chants, fire textures, water (escape), bird (outro)
• Dynamics: MAXIMUM from beat one → relentless (V1) → peak (C) → urgent running (V2) → TOTAL SILENCE (bridge) → instruments dying (FC) → gentle valley (outro)
• Hook Type: "Dawnwatch is falling" (title hook) + "Surviving's just — not dying yet" (thesis)
• Rhyme Scheme: V=ABCB, PC=AABB, C=ABAB, Bridge=ABCB (quiet ballad for max devastation)
• Emotional Arc: Chaos → losing → flight → Lyra's death → escape → exhausted survival → dawn
