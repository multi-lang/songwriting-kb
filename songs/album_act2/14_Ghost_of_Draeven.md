# Ghost of Draeven — Album Track 14 (v3)

> Song Thesis: *You can't fight the enemy outside until you face the one you used to be —
> redemption starts with refusing to look away from your own hands.*

---

## STYLE PROMPT:
Dark psychological cinematic ballad, haunted introspective rock, male baritone A2-E4, key C minor, 4/4, 74 BPM, sparse piano, deep cello, mountain wind ambience, emotional strings building, minimal percussion until final chorus, intimate verses opening to vast choruses, confessional, raw emotional vulnerability

[Exclusions: ‑bright synths, ‑pop energy, ‑trap drums, ‑EDM drops, ‑beatboxing, ‑vocal hums, ‑cheerful melodies, ‑autotune, ‑aggressive metal, ‑industrial textures, ‑fast tempo, ‑war drums]

---

## LYRICS:

[Title: Ghost of Draeven]

[Production Direction:
Most INTERNAL song. No external threat — the danger is memory. Wind + single piano note — Rikan at the summit, alone with his past. Verses claustrophobic. Choruses open vast. Bridge: internal dialogue — two vocal registers (main close/dry, parenthetical distant/reverbed). Final chorus: resolved with purpose. Target 4:30.]

[Vocal Direction:
Male baritone — most vulnerable on the album. Verses: confessional, close, ashamed. Pre-chorus cracks. Chorus: full aching — not screaming, ACHING. Bridge: internal dialogue — main voice close-mic, parenthetical voice distant/lower register. Final chorus: scarred but standing.]

[Intro - The Summit]
[Mountain wind] [Single piano note] [Nexus hum]

The crystal pulses at the summit
The wind carries voices I don't want to hear
The enemy isn't only out there
The enemy was me — once

[Verse 1 - The Memories]
[Piano sparse, minor] [Close-mic] [Cello drone enters]

I remember the ritual now — in flashes
A circle drawn in someone else's blood
I remember the seductive promise
Power beyond the limits of the flesh

The faces come at night — I can't unsee them
The ones who suffered because I was weak
I was the weapon before I was the hero
I was the harm before I found the Mark

[Pre-Chorus - The Weight]
[Cello swells] [Strings — aching]

How do you carry what you've done
When you're both the wound and what it made
How do you ask for their forgiveness
When you can't forgive the face inside your face

[Chorus - The Ghost]
[Strings full — emotional, wide] [Piano powerful] [Mountain reverb]

Ghost of Draeven standing at my back
Wearing all the faces that I wrecked
I can feel him like a second shadow
The man I was — the man I can't forget

Ghost of Draeven — haunting every choice
Am I different — or just dressed in better armor
The same destruction wearing new flesh

I can't outrun what I've already been
I can only choose what happens next

[Verse 2 - Facing It]
[Piano + cello] [Strings sustain] [Vocal more present]

Alaric knows — he's always known
He saw the ghost of Draeven in my eyes
He trained me anyway — not blind to what I was
But choosing to believe in what I'd try

The Nexus core reflects my face back wrong
It shows me both — the Ratfolk and the man
The code writer and the catastrophe
Both beating in one heart

[Pre-Chorus 2]
[Strings darker] [Cello deeper] [Wind stronger]

If I close the Rift — does that erase
The cracks I helped to make
Or do I carry both forever
The redemption and the scar

[Bridge - The Reckoning]
[Everything drops — wind + single cello] [Internal dialogue]

Was I taken — or did I go willingly
(you went willingly)

Did I know what I was doing
(you knew enough — you chose not to look)

Am I different now
(you're different — and you're the same)

Can I be forgiven
(not by them — only by what you do next)

(you stand up)
(you face the wind)
(you carry both names)
(and you MOVE)

[Final Chorus - Rising]
[Strings return — warmer] [Piano with purpose] [Heartbeat percussion enters]

Ghost of Draeven — I see you now
You're not my enemy — you're my foundation
Every terrible thing that I allowed
Became the reason I won't let it happen

Ghost of Draeven — you can stay
I won't pretend you never lived
But you don't steer anymore — I do
And where I'm going needs what suffering taught

I can't undo — I can't erase
But I can stand at this impossible height
And be the one who breaks the cycle — not who made it

Ghost of Draeven — rest now
I'll carry us both from here

[Outro - Descent]
[Piano gentles] [Cello resolves warm] [Wind calms]

The weight is still here
But it sits differently now
Not on my shoulders
But in my spine — holding me up

## PRODUCTION NOTES:

• Key: C minor — emotional, classical weight, inner conflict key
• Tempo: 74 BPM (contemplative, standing still and facing something)
• Time Signature: 4/4
• Chord Progression:
  Verse: i – VII – VI – v (Cm – Bb – Ab – Gm) [descending = sinking into memory]
  Pre-Chorus: iv – i – VII – III (Fm – Cm – Bb – Eb) [reaching toward hope, pulled back]
  Chorus: i – VI – VII – iv (Cm – Ab – Bb – Fm) [wide, aching]
  Bridge: i sustained (Cm → resolving with purpose)
  Final Chorus: VI – VII – i – III (Ab – Bb – Cm – Eb) [arrives at Eb more confidently = acceptance]
• Vocal: Male baritone (A2–E4), ashamed → cracking → full anguish → dialogue → resolved
• Instruments: Sparse piano, cello (emotional weight), strings, mountain wind, Nexus hum, heartbeat percussion (FC only)
• Dynamics: Wind + note (intro) → piano whisper (V1) → cello adds (PC) → strings wide (C) → intimate (V2) → stripped (bridge) → rebuilt warm (FC) → gentle (outro)
• Hook Type: "Ghost of Draeven" (title) + "I can only choose what happens next" (thesis)
• Rhyme Scheme: V=ABCB, PC=AABB, C=ABAB, Bridge=Free (dialogue)
• Emotional Arc: Guilt → memories → confrontation → internal dialogue → acceptance → forward with purpose
