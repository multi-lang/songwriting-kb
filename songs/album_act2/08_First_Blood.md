# First Blood — Album Track 8 (v3)

> Song Thesis: *The first time you discover what your new body can do, you realize
> you're no longer the person who would have flinched.*

---

## STYLE PROMPT:
Dark aggressive cinematic rock, primal combat energy, male baritone A2-E4, key B minor, 4/4, 96 BPM, heavy tribal percussion, distorted bass, cave reverb atmosphere, sharp staccato guitar riffs, industrial undertones fading into organic aggression, primal drums building through song, whispered paranoia verses into explosive choruses, fantasy combat intensity, visceral and physical, no clean polish

[Exclusions: ‑bright synths, ‑pop vocals, ‑upbeat energy, ‑EDM drops, ‑cheerful melodies, ‑trap drums, ‑beatboxing, ‑vocal hums, ‑autotune, ‑clean production, ‑heroic triumphant feeling, ‑orchestral bombast]

## LYRICS:

[Title: First Blood]

[Production Direction:
Continues from Track 7's cave ambience. Verse 1 is near-silent: breath, heartbeat, distant scrabbling. Goblins trigger a shift — primal drums enter, distorted bass, the cave becomes a weapon. Chorus explodes with aggressive energy but stays CONTAINED — discovery, not triumph. Bridge drops to aftermath silence. Final chorus is darker, conflicted. Production should feel like discovering a machine inside yourself.]

[Vocal Direction:
Male baritone — whispered and terrified in intro/verse 1. As instinct takes over, voice hardens, becomes predatory. Chorus: powerful but strained — a man discovering lethal precision. Bridge: near-spoken, hollow, staring at his hands. Final chorus: conflicted strength acknowledged with unease.]

[Intro - The Dark]
[Wet cave ambience] [Heartbeat] [Distant scrabbling]

Something in the dark
Something watching

Three pairs of red — getting closer

[Whispered]
(I've never killed anything)

[Verse 1 - Prey]
[Near silence] [Heartbeat kick] [Cave reverb] [Whispered vocal]

Pressed against the stone — lungs burning
Smell of rot and rust and something sweet
Red eyes in the black — three sets advancing
Crude blades catching light I shouldn't see

My nerves say run — something disagrees
Something deeper waking underneath
Muscles I don't recognize are coiling
Claws I didn't ask for find their reach

[Pre-Chorus - The Switch]
[Tribal drums begin — low, primal] [Bass pulse enters] [Vocal hardens]

I didn't choose this body
But the body chose this fight

Something ancient in my spine
Already knows the strike

[Chorus - Predator]
[Heavy tribal drums] [Distorted bass] [Staccato guitar riff] [Cave reverb on vocal]

First blood on my claws tonight
Moved before I made the choice
Something faster than my mind
Used my hands — used my voice

First blood — and the dark went quiet
First blood — and my body sang
I was prey a breath ago
Now the cave knows my name

[Post-Chorus]
[Drums pull back] [Low resonant hum]

I didn't hesitate
(why didn't I hesitate)

[Verse 2 - The Kill]
[Drums return — harder] [Bass drives] [Guitar texture thickens]

Circled in the shadows — found the gap
Claws against a throat before it screamed
The second fell mid-lunge — I caught its weight
And turned its force against itself

Three bodies on the stone — the silence after
Three lives I ended with a stranger's hands
The exhilaration hits before the horror
I understand before I understand

[Pre-Chorus 2 - Recognition]
[Heavier percussion] [Industrial edge] [Vocal strain]

These aren't programmed reflexes
This is what I've become

The code became the muscle
And the muscle drew the blood

[Bridge - Aftermath]
[Everything drops] [Single drip] [Near-spoken vocal]

Blood drying on my claws
Thick and dark and real

I made them stop
I made them stop

I sat behind a desk
Never broke a thing I couldn't fix with code

Now I'm standing in the dark
With someone else's blood
And the worst part is how natural it felt

(the worst part is I'd do it again)

[Final Chorus - Conflicted Power]
[Full drums — darker, heavier] [Bass wall] [Guitar aggressive but controlled]

First blood on my claws tonight
Something woke that won't go back to sleep
I was Draeven at a keyboard once
Now I'm whatever this world needs to be

First blood — and the guilt tastes copper
First blood — and my body knows
I was someone else before this moment
But that someone couldn't survive this world

First blood
First blood

The cave remembers what I did
And so do I

[Outro - Moving Deeper]
[Cave ambience returns] [Heartbeat steadies] [Obsidian shard pulse]

Forward — deeper — into the dark

The shard against my chest
Is pulling me somewhere

And I follow

## PRODUCTION NOTES:

• Key: B minor — aggressive, dark, physical, guitar-driven
• Tempo: 96 BPM (combat pace — elevated heartbeat, controlled aggression)
• Time Signature: 4/4
• Chord Progression:
  Verse: i – VII – VI – VII (Bm – A – G – A) [stalking, predatory momentum]
  Pre-Chorus: iv – VII – i – V (Em – A – Bm – F#) [tension, V creates urgency]
  Chorus: i – VII – III – iv (Bm – A – D – Em) [D major = unexpected brightness = the thrill]
  Bridge: i – i – iv – iv (Bm – Bm – Em – Em) [static, trapped in the moment]
  Final Chorus: i – VII – VI – v (Bm – A – G – F#m) [descending = gravity, weight]
• Vocal: Male baritone (A2–E4), terrified whisper → hardening → predatory → hollow → conflicted
• Instruments: Cave ambience, heartbeat percussion, heavy tribal drums, distorted bass, staccato guitar riff, industrial undertones (fading), low resonant hum
• Dynamics: Near-silence (intro) → whispered tension (V1) → drums enter (PC) → explosive (C) → silence (post-C) → driving (V2) → total drop (bridge) → dark power (FC) → shard pulse (outro)
• Hook Type: Title hook "First blood" + identity claim "the cave knows my name"
• Rhyme Scheme: V=ABCB, PC=ABAB, C=ABCB, Bridge=Free
• Emotional Arc: Terror → instinct → violence as discovery → exhilaration → horror → guilt → resigned acceptance → forward
