# Campfire — Album Track 16 (v3)

> Song Thesis: *You don't choose your family by blood — you choose them by
> who's still sitting next to you when the fire burns low.*

---

## STYLE PROMPT:
Warm indie folk rock, intimate campfire atmosphere, male baritone A2-D4, key D major, 4/4, 84 BPM, fingerpicked acoustic guitar, warm bass, gentle brushed drums, soft mandolin accents, crackling fire ambience, low cello warmth, earned tenderness, bittersweet hope, the warmest song on the album

[Exclusions: ‑dark atmosphere, ‑heavy distortion, ‑trap drums, ‑EDM drops, ‑beatboxing, ‑vocal hums, ‑industrial textures, ‑cold production, ‑aggressive energy, ‑autotune, ‑orchestral bombast, ‑fast tempo]

## LYRICS:

[Title: Campfire]

[Production Direction:
WARMEST track. Deliberate exhale. Fire crackle constant (low, comforting). Fingerpicked acoustic foundation. Bass enters V2. Brushed drums at pre-chorus. Mandolin in choruses (Celtic warmth — Kael nod). Bridge strips to fire + voice for Lyra's absence. Final chorus: fullest, warmest on album. Target 4:00.]

[Vocal Direction:
Male baritone — warmest, most relaxed vocal. Man at rest, surrounded by trust. Verses: conversational. Pre-chorus: tender. Chorus: open-hearted, gentle. Bridge: aching. Final chorus: closest to joy on the album.]

[Intro - The Fire]
[Crackling fire] [Fingerpicked acoustic — simple, warm]

The fire finds the faces of the ones who stayed
Nobody's talking — but nobody needs to
This silence isn't empty — it's full

[Verse 1 - Kael]
[Acoustic fingerpick] [Fire crackle] [Warm vocal]

Kael sits closest to the flame — he always does
His hands wrapped round a cup like they wrapped stone
The night the tunnel tried to crush us flat
He held the ceiling up until we'd gone

He never said a word about it after
A granite cliff I've leaned against so often
I stopped noticing I was leaning
Just felt held

[Verse 2 - Elara]
[Bass enters] [Guitar continues] [Mandolin distant]

Elara reads the stars like others read a page
And somehow knows the thing I need before I speak
She told me once — in the quiet after fighting
"The truest strength is knowing when you're wrong"

I didn't understand until the summit
Now I carry that beside me like a song
Her magic isn't just the arcane kind
It's knowing when to push — and when to hold

[Pre-Chorus - Gratitude]
[Brushed drums enter] [Cello beneath]

I never chose these people — they chose me
Before I'd earned it — still they held their ground
And everything I couldn't carry solo
They lifted without making any sound

[Chorus - The Bonds]
[Full acoustic] [Drums] [Bass] [Mandolin] [Cello]

Here around the fire — we're still breathing
Scarred and tired — but we're still here
The world outside is dark and full of enemies
But in this circle — nothing left to fear

These bonds weren't forged in comfort
They were forged in blood and tunnels and escape
And somehow that's the most honest love I've known

Home isn't a place — home is who stays

[Bridge - Her Space]
[Everything drops — fire and voice only] [Whispered]

There's a space beside the fire where she'd sit
Her jokes would fill the silence I can't fill
Lyra's absence is a sound — not silence
It's the gap where laughter used to land

She'd hate this — the solemnity
She'd throw a stick at Kael — call him dramatic
And the grief would break into a laugh

I miss her — and I carry her beside the others
Not as weight — but as warmth I won't let go

[Final Chorus - Full Circle]
[Everything returns — FULLEST warmth] [Mandolin, cello, guitar, drums]

Here around the fire — we're still breathing
Broken pieces leaning into each other's cracks
The world outside is vast and wants to end us
But it hasn't yet — because of this

Here around the fire — I'm not alone
For the first time since the transfer took my life
I have people — scarred and stubborn people
Who would walk into the dark if I asked twice

Home isn't a place — home is who stays
Home is who stays

[Outro - Embers]
[Guitar single notes] [Fire dying] [Night sounds]

The fire burns low — Kael's already snoring
Elara watches something in the east
I stay awake a little longer
Not from fear — but gratitude

Tomorrow we fight again
But tonight — resting together — is enough

## PRODUCTION NOTES:

• Key: D major — WARMEST key on album. First major key. Earned after 15 minor tracks.
• Tempo: 84 BPM (relaxed, restful)
• Time Signature: 4/4
• Chord Progression:
  Verse: I – V – vi – IV (D – A – Bm – G) [universal warmth]
  Pre-Chorus: vi – IV – I – V (Bm – G – D – A) [bittersweet → resolves]
  Chorus: I – V – IV – I (D – A – G – D) [simplest, open, plagal amen = peace]
  Bridge: vi – IV – I – V (Bm – G – D – A) [Lyra's space — minor but resolves warm]
  Final Chorus: I – V – IV – V – I (D – A – G – A – D) [fullest, most resolved = HOME]
• Vocal: Male baritone (A2–D4), warmest delivery, conversational → tender → aching → joyful
• Instruments: Fingerpicked acoustic (constant), upright bass, brushed drums, mandolin (Celtic), warm cello, fire crackle, night sounds
• Dynamics: Fire + guitar (intro) → guitar + voice (V1) → + bass (V2) → + drums/cello (PC) → full warm (C) → stripped fire + voice (bridge) → FULLEST (FC) → embers (outro)
• Hook Type: "Here around the fire" (setting hook) + "Home isn't a place — home is who stays" (thesis)
• Rhyme Scheme: V=ABCB (folk storytelling), PC=ABCB, C=AABB + tag, Bridge=Free
• Emotional Arc: Gratitude → warmth → belonging → Lyra's absence → carrying her warmly → fullest belonging
