## Lyrics

[Title: Shapes Behind My Eyes]

[Production Direction: Dark, intimate, and atmospheric. Begin near-silent with single piano note and breath, bloom into fingerpicked guitar, add ambient pads and reverb in choruses, strip to heartbeat in bridge, rebuild with warmth and gentle strings for final chorus. Never aggressive. Proxemic arc: start in-your-ear intimate, open to social distance in choruses, collapse to primal heartbeat in bridge, rebuild to warm public space in final chorus. Piano and guitar are truth anchors. Pads are the unknown pressing in. Choir pad appears ONLY in final chorus as acceptance/spiritual warmth. Heartbeat percussion in bridge only — primal, ancient, grounding. The sound of meeting yourself in the dark and discovering love there.]

## Revision Notes

### Issue 1: Stuffed V2 line (FIXED)
- **Was:** "Or living a story the universe already scripted" (14 syllables)
- **Now:** "Or reading lines the universe wrote first" (10 syllables)
- **Why:** Physical verb ("reading lines"), maintains the determinism concept, singable at 72 BPM

### Issue 2: "Old focus press my mind" — abstract (FIXED)
- **Was:** "Until I feel an old focus press my mind"
- **Now:** "Until old fingers press against my mind"
- **Why:** Personification — "fingers" is physical, creepy, specific. Maintains the "old" quality and "press" action but grounds it in a body-sensation. Connects to the "face that wears my mouth" imagery in V1 (a presence with physical features).

### Issue 3: Identical Chorus 2 (FIXED)
- **Was:** Word-for-word identical to Chorus 1
- **Now:** Two key line changes:
  - "Something moves" → "Something **builds**" (escalation from V2's intensification)
  - "Or remembering where I'm from" → "Or **becoming** where I'm from" (stakes raised — not just memory but transformation)
  - "In a voice that sounds like me" → "And the voice — **it knows my name**" (the shapes are no longer anonymous; they recognize HIM — sets up bridge revelation)
- **Why:** C1 = questioning the unknown. C2 = the unknown is getting closer/personal. FC = acceptance. Three-stage chorus evolution.

### Issue 4: Bridge tightened (FIXED)
- **Was:** 12 lines (4 whispered/setup + 8 sung)
- **Now:** 6 lines (1 whispered + 5 sung)
- **Cut:** "You didn't know — but I was never gone / When you shut your eyes I come alive" — these are setup lines whose content is now IMPLICIT in the soul-voice simply arriving and speaking with familiarity. The "I've always been here" line covers the "never gone" concept. Tighter = more impact.
- **Why:** The bridge now hits in ~25-30 seconds instead of ~50. The revelation is immediate and emotionally concentrated.

### Issue 5: "Movie scroll" metaphor (FIXED)
- **Was:** "This feels so old — like a movie scroll"
- **Now:** "This feels so old — like a film reel burning at the fold"
- **Why:** Specific (film reel = tangible object), sensory (burning = visual/tactile), "at the fold" implies degradation at the crease-point — something watched so many times it's damaged. The line now SHOWS age rather than vaguely gesturing at it.

### Issue 6: Bridge voice differentiation (FIXED)
- **Was:** No production cue distinguishing soul-voice from narrator
- **Now:** `[Warm, slightly reverbed vocal — deeper register, gentle authority]` added before the soul speaks
- **Why:** In Suno rendering this signals a register shift, reverb change, and delivery change — the listener HEARS a different voice. The narrator is "close-mic, breathy, cracked" while the soul is "warm, reverbed, deeper, authoritative but gentle." This makes The Turn sonically undeniable.
