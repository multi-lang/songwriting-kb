## Strongest Line

> "For the silence that we give our friends"

Reframes the entire song retroactively. The "quiet" in every chorus ("when it all goes quiet — that's the work we bring") is revealed as a GIFT given at personal cost — not just professional competence, but an act of generosity. "Friends" (not "clients" or "users") humanizes the relationship. The line transforms the hook from a competence statement into a sacrifice statement on every subsequent listen.

---

## Any Remaining Issues?

The song is clean. The "who we am" fix landed naturally ("where we stand"), Greg/Danny lines sing properly, pre-choruses are now genuinely surprising, all tell-language is eliminated, and the structure is tight. 

**One micro-observation (not a flag):** Chorus 2 line 2 breaks the rhyme pattern of C1 — "Every pixel mapped — every threshold ring" doesn't rhyme with "fall" the way "From the smallest signal — to the biggest call" does. This is likely intentional (swagger section asserting independence from the pattern), and it works — but note it as a conscious choice rather than an oversight. The variation HELPS differentiation between choruses, so this is a feature, not a bug.
