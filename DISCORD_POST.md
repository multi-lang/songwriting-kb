## Full Version

---

**🎵 Songwriting Knowledge Base — Free Kiro Package**

I built a professional songwriting system for Kiro that packages everything I've learned about AI-assisted song production into one cloneable repo. It works in Kiro IDE or kiro-cli.

**What it does:**
- Write complete songs from a concept (Nashville method — chorus first)
- Score songs on a 12-category rubric backed by academic musicology
- Format for Suno v5.5 with proper meta-tags, layers, and char limits
- Manage concept album continuity (track registries, hard rules, motifs)

**What's inside:**
- 4 agents (songwriter, critic, suno-optimizer, album-continuity)
- 5 skills (music theory, character voice, critique framework, Suno tags, album bible)
- 5 hooks (prosody lint, format check, char count, quick score button, full pipeline button)
- 8 SOPs covering every workflow from scratch to finished album
- Complete knowledge base: 12 academic disciplines, 23-point production framework, 35+ confirmed Suno tags

**How it works:**
```
git clone https://github.com/Voletek/songwriting-kb.git
```
Open in Kiro. The `.kiro/` directory auto-configures everything. Then just talk:
- "Write a song about loneliness after a breakup"
- "Critique this song"
- "Make this Suno-ready"
- "Check album continuity"

**The 9-step workflow it applies to every song:**
Semantic → Emotional → Prosodic → Narrative → Voice → Genre → Arrangement → Production → Commercial

**Quality gates enforced automatically:**
- Hook within 15 seconds of first vocal
- No line over 12 syllables
- V2 adds new information
- Bridge contains a genuine turn
- Named parenthetical layers declared in Style Prompt

**Who it's for:**
- Songwriters using Suno (or any AI music tool)
- Anyone building concept albums who needs continuity tracking
- People who want structured methodology instead of vibes-based prompting
- Works for any genre — the framework is universal, conventions adapt

**Customizable:**
The system separates universal craft (works for everyone) from personal config (your album, your format preferences). Two complete example albums included as reference implementations. SOP 08 walks you through setup.

**Not just for Suno:**
The songwriting methodology, critique rubric, and production theory work for human performance, other AI tools, or traditional recording. The Suno-specific pieces are clearly labeled and optional.

🔗 https://github.com/Voletek/songwriting-kb

Free to use, customize, and share. PRs welcome for new genre conventions, Suno tag findings, or additional skills.
